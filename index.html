Hi, my name's Josh. I'm currently based out of Fort Worth. I've had a somewhat eclectic career as a trainer, instructional designer, and videographer, sometimes doing all three at one time within a single position.

In terms of video production, I have worked on features, documentaries, shorts, music videos, and a plethora of marketing and training videos, doing just about everything, including directing and editing along with lighting and photography.

In my capacities as a trainer and instructional designer, I have developed training for the likes of Apple and have personally trained something north of 10,000 individual students. I can hardly believe it myself, but somehow it happened.

This site is a collection showcasing just a few of the things I've worked on over the years. If you'd like to know more about my work or preview client work that I cannot share publicly, please reach out to me at joshuacornelius [at] mac [dot] com. My resume is available on request.
