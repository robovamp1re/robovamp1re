I've been working as a videographer for many years, starting with a 90 minute documentary I created for my family detailing my sisters senior year of high school. The first actual production I was invited to step foot on was a feature film starring David Naughton of An American Werewolf in London fame. Through an insane series of events I was promoted from production assistant to gaffer... with absolutely no lighting experience to speak of. Regardless, I worked with the director of photography to achieve the desired look, which centered heavily on a night club setting in which we needed to carefully rig a ridiculous number of lights so that they might not come crashing down on the heads of our 50 dancing extras.

Since then I've worked on documentaries, shorts, music videos, in a variety of roles, and I have shot, written, and edited countless marketing and training related videos for clients. What you see here are a smattering of the videos I've worked on, some new, and some a little more retro.
